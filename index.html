Indonesia Is My Place

Indonesia is the world's largest tropical archipelago, strategically located between Asia and Australia, and the Indian and Pacific Oceans, known for its extraordinary biodiversity, rich multi-ethnic culture with the motto "Bhinneka Tunggal Ika", the world's largest Muslim population, and a rapidly growing economy.

Most Visited

Bali Island is a small beautiful island and a part of Indonesia's archipelago, and the most famous of Indonesian tourism in the world.

Most Visited

Jakarta is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in Southeast Asia. Established in the fourth century as Sunda Kelapa, the city became an important trading port for the Sunda Kingdom.

Most Visited

Papua is the easternmost province of Indonesia on the island of Papua, famous for its extraordinary natural wealth, diverse tribal cultures with unique traditions such as stone burning, and abundant natural resources such as gold and copper.

Activities You Should Do

Visit Komodo Island

Visit the colossal Buddhist temple of Borobudur in Java at sunrise for an unforgettable spiritual experience, or explore the intricate Hindu temples of Prambanan nearby for a deep dive into Indonesian history and architecture.

Volcanic Landscapes & Sunrises (Mt. Bromo)

Hike or take a jeep tour up Mount Bromo in East Java to witness an otherworldly sunrise over its vast, smoky caldera, a truly iconic Indonesian sight.

Raja Ampat, Papua

Explore world-class coral reefs, swim with manta rays, reef sharks, turtles, and a vast array of fish.
